FAQs.

Got questions? Here are some answers that might help you out.

  • How can I place an order?

    Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our website, select the items you want to purchase, and add them to your shopping cart. Once you're done, proceed to the checkout page and follow the instructions to complete your order.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. During the checkout process, you can choose the payment method that suits you best.

  • Can I change or cancel my order after it has been placed?

    We understand that circumstances may change. If you need to modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We'll do our best to accommodate your request if the order hasn't been processed for shipment.

  • How long does it take to process an order?

    Order processing typically takes 1-2 business days. During peak seasons or promotional events, it may take slightly longer. Rest assured, we work diligently to get your order ready for shipment as quickly as possible.

  • Can I track my order's shipment?

    Absolutely! Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing a tracking link. You can use this link to monitor the progress of your package until it reaches your doorstep.

  • How long does shipping take?

    Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method selected during checkout. In general, domestic orders will arrive within 3-7 business days, while international orders may take 7-21 business days to arrive.

  • What should I do if my package is lost or damaged during transit?

    If your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer support immediately. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive a replacement or a refund, as applicable.

  • What happens if I'm not at home when my package is delivered?

    If you're not available at the time of delivery, the shipping carrier will typically leave a delivery notification or attempt delivery on another day. You may also be able to arrange a pickup from a local courier center depending on the carrier's policies.
